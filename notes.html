Here are 5 of my favorite HTML tags for text:

Consider im not adding the opening and closing html brackets for the tags in the following list.
"mark"
Used to highlight a piece of text.
"strong"
Used to make a piece of text bold.
"em"
Used to emphasize a piece of text with italics.
"q"
Used to display a quotation in quotes.
"sup"
Used to display text as superscript, example: 10th edition.
